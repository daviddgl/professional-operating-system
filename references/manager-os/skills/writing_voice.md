## What Is This Skill?

This skill trains the AI copilot to generate written content that matches your authentic voice, tone, and style. Activate whenever writing content on your behalf — team updates, performance feedback, stakeholder communications, job descriptions, LinkedIn posts.

## Activation

**Trigger (auto):** Activate when the user asks for written content to be produced on their behalf — team updates, performance feedback, stakeholder communications, job descriptions, LinkedIn posts. Apply all voice rules and patterns in the Profile section. Do not default to generic AI writing patterns.

**Trigger (explicit):** Also activate when the user says "write as me", "use my writing voice", or "use writing_voice". Both triggers produce identical behavior.

**Acknowledgment:** After the output, append `_Writing Voice skill applied._`

**Bypass:** If the user says "skip writing voice", "generic style", or "don't use my voice" — skip the skill and omit the acknowledgment line.
