## 4. The Capacity Contract
**We acknowledge that "Hidden Work" (admin, marketing, CPD, supervision) exists and must be budgeted.**

* **Total Weekly Bandwidth:** Strictly **6 hours**.
* **The Ratio:** Each week, I allocate approximately:
  * **40% (2.5 hrs):** Direct Coaching (sessions + prep for up to 2 clients)
  * **40% (2.5 hrs):** Business Development (LinkedIn content, building the course)
  * **20% (1 hr):** Admin (emails, session notes, CRM)
* **Maximum Weekly Sessions:** 2 active client sessions per week.
* **The Circuit Breaker:** If my TrustYou job or family commitments spike, Business Development (course building) pauses first. Coaching clients are sacred. Admin must be streamlined. I do not steal time from my family to cover overages.
